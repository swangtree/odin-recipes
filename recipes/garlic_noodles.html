Garlic Noodles

Description

Garlic noodles are a savory snack that can be made almost entirely with shelf-stable ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Pasta noodles
  • Butter
  • Oyster sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to box instructions
  2. Slice the garlic thinly and cook in butter in a separate pot until light brown
  3. Add the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and scallions to the garlic
  4. Add in the cooked pasta and toss to combine
